My 1st Game Project

T-rex Runner

Trex runner game is a running dinosaur compatible with all mobile phones. Jump the dino trex over the cacti to gain points. To start the game, press "Space" or tap on the screen which will also jump the Trex dino. Keep tapping to jump the dino trex any time over the cacti and other hurdles that come along.
